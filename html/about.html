About Us

You Imagine it, We Build it.

CompBuilt simply provides a platform for consumers to conceptualize, customize, and purchase desktop computers with the option of having professional support and recommendations. Buying a customized desktop computer should not be a process that takes hundreds of hours of research - CompBuilt aims to remove the hassle and provide consumers a risk-free and easy way for them to customize their future desktop computers.

For Everyone

Whether you are a gamer, creator, or the average joe, you can build a custom computer. CompBuilt is for everyone who wants or needs a custom computer and may not have the resources or ability to build one - we’ll help you. We believe that everyone should have access to the knowledge, resources, and the ability to easily create and build custom computers.

Inspired by the Future

We are inspired to create and build for the future. We believe humans are only limited by the tools they have for the mind has infinite potential. We want to further remove the barriers between machine and man and we are starting with custom-built computers. Yes we sell computer parts and computer builds but in the process, we drive forward the world to a better technological future.

Risk-free

Don’t risk losing your money by blindly buying computer parts and building it on your own. We believe you can do it but we can eliminate the hassle and also include you in the journey of building your concept computer! You don’t need hours of research to have your own custom PC - we can make it work. Just you and us.

No Hassle

You imagine the build, you create it on our platform, you order it, and we ship it. All that in just a few clicks - no hundreds of research or experimenting with the risk of breaking your computer parts (ever heard of fried motherboards?).

Dubai, UAE

CompBuilt is based and operating business in Dubai but we cover the international market. Worldwide shipping and support is available. Dubai is one of the rising business & innovation hub in the world which we believe could help us grow and improve CompBuilt to it’s maximum potential.

Expert in the Field

CompBuilt is composed of a team of highly skilled and expert computer enthusiasts who built computers over the past few years. We have the talent and skills required to help you with everything you need about custom computers - whether you’re a pro or not.

Worldwide Shipping

We offer worldwide shipping with your custom computer packed in crates. You even have the option to ship your custom computer to be built by us first or have it packed in their original boxes and build it yourself. Of course, you’ll still get the support and customer care from us.

Warranty & Returns

All the computer components comes with manufacturers’ warranty which is usually 1 year from the date of purchase. We don’t offer returns if you choose to let us build your concept computer. Facing a problem with your build? Let us know and contact us for support and after-market services. One of our experts will be in contact with you and help you realtime in solving your issues.
